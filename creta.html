Creta Latest Update


Latest update: Hyundai now offers the base-spec Creta with a petrol engine making it more affordable than before while raising prices of all other variants too. See new variant wise prices here. Hyundai Creta Price: Hyundai has priced the Creta 2020 from Rs 9.82 lakh to Rs 17.32 lakh (ex-showroom India). Hyundai Creta Variants: It is available in five variants: E, EX, S, SX, and SX(O). Read more about them here. Hyundai Creta Seating Capacity: The Creta 2020 is offered in a 5 seater layout. Hyundai Creta Powertrains: The Hyundai SUV shares its BS6 engines with the Kia Seltos: a 1.5-litre naturally aspirated petrol (115PS/144Nm), a 1.5-litre diesel (115PS/250Nm), and a 1.4-litre turbo-petrol (140PS/242Nm). A 6-speed manual transmission is offered as standard with the 1.5-litre petrol and diesel engines. The 1.5-litre petrol engine can also be had with the option of a CVT, whereas the diesel engine is offered with a 6-speed torque converter. The 1.4-litre turbo-petrol comes with a 7-speed DCT (dual-clutch automatic) only. The Creta 2020 is offered with a 2WD system. Hyundai Creta Features: It comes with LED headlamps and tail lamps, panoramic sunroof, wireless charging, power-adjustable driver’s seat, and ventilated front seats. It also gets a 7-inch semi-digital instrument cluster and a 10.25-inch touchscreen infotainment system along with connected car tech. Hyundai Creta Safety: Safety features on offer include up to six airbags, ABS with EBD, and rear parking sensors. Hyundai Creta Rivals: The Creta 2020 fights it out with the Kia Seltos, Renault Captur, Nissan Kicks, and the upcoming Volkswagen Taigun and Skoda Vision IN. With its premium pricing, its top variants face competition from the likes of the Tata Harrier and MG Hector. If you are looking for a more rugged SUV, you can check out the Mahindra Scorpio. Hyundai Creta 7 seater: Hyundai is currently developing a 7 seater version of the Creta. It could be called the ‘Alcazar’, which was trademarked recently.


Hyundai Creta Price


The price of Hyundai Creta starts at Rs. 9.81 Lakh and goes upto Rs. 17.31 Lakh. Hyundai Creta is offered in 17 variants - the base model of Creta is E and the top variant Hyundai Creta SX Opt Diesel AT which comes at a price tag of Rs. 17.31 Lakh.
